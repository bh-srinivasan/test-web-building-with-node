The Art of Abstract

with Riley Rewington

Meet Riley Rewington, a Seattle-based college student and emerging artist. Riley Rewington has always had a passion for creating, and has been honing their skills in various mediums, including painting, drawing, and sculpture. As a student at Stanford, Riley Rewington is currently studying Medicine, but continues to make art a central part of their life. Riley Rewington has had their work exhibited in galleries throughout Seattle, and has won several awards for their pieces. They are particularly interested in exploring themes of identity and societal structures in their art, and use a mix of traditional and experimental techniques to convey their message. In addition to being an artist, Riley Rewington is also an active member of the art community in Seattle. They volunteer at local art programs for underprivileged youth, and are a member of a collective of emerging artists. Riley Rewington is excited to share their passion and knowledge of the art world with others, and is looking forward to speaking at [event name] about their experiences as a college student and artist.
